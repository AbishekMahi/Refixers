Our Services

We offers a comprehensive range of home repair and maintenance services, from small repairs to major renovations. Our skilled technicians are dedicated to providing the highest quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.

Door Refixing

Our expert technicians are skilled in fixing all types of doors, including UPVC doors, glass doors, and wooden doors. Whether your door is sticking, sagging, or simply in need of a new handle, we can get it working smoothly again in no time.

Windows Refixing

From broken panes to worn weather stripping, our technicians have the expertise to fix any window issue. We can replace glass, repair frames, and install new locks and hardware to ensure your windows are working properly and efficiently.

Kitchens & Wardrobes Refixing

The busiest spots in any home, kitchens and wardrobes require regular maintenance to stay functional and organized. We can repair or replace hinges, drawers, shelves, and more to ensure your kitchen and wardrobe are working at their best.

Handles Refixing

If your door or cabinet handles are loose, sticking, or broken, our technicians can help. We can repair or replace handles to ensure they are functioning properly and securely.

Lamination Refixing

Laminate surfaces can become damaged over time, making them less attractive and less durable. We can repair or replace laminate surfaces, restoring them to their original beauty and functionality.

Hinges Refixing

Hinges can become loose or rusty, making doors difficult to open and close. Our technicians can repair or replace hinges to ensure your doors are functioning properly and smoothly.

Key Refixing & Change

Whether you need a new key made or your existing key repaired, our technicians can help. We can also change your locks and provide new keys to ensure your home is secure and your belongings are safe.

Lock Refixing

A broken lock can be a serious security issue. Our technicians can repair or replace locks to ensure your home is secure and protected.
